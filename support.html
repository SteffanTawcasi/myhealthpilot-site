MyHealthPilot Support

MyHealthPilot is a support tool for symptom organisation, research translation, and monitoring. It is not a diagnostic or emergency medical service.

Contact support

Email: myhealthpilotmobile@gmail.com

What to include in your message

To help us respond faster, include:

  • Your device type
  • Your app version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots if relevant

Help topics

  • Account sign in or sign up issues
  • AI consent questions
  • Record upload problems
  • Monitoring plan or check-in issues
  • Reminder notification issues
  • Privacy or account deletion questions

Emergency notice

If you think you may be experiencing a medical emergency, do not rely on MyHealthPilot. Contact local emergency services or a licensed clinician immediately.
