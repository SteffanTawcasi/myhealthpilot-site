MyHealthPilot Account Deletion

You can request account deletion directly inside the MyHealthPilot app.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open MyHealthPilot
  2. Go to the Account tab
  3. Scroll to the Danger zone
  4. Tap Delete account
  5. Confirm deletion

What happens when you delete your account

MyHealthPilot will attempt to delete your Firebase account and clear related local app data stored on your device, including profile details, chat state, plans, reports, reminders, and AI consent records.

If you cannot access the app

If you cannot sign in to access deletion inside the app, email: myhealthpilotmobile@gmail.com
