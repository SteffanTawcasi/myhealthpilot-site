MyHealthPilot Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 1, 2026

MyHealthPilot helps users organise symptom information, translate research and guidance into plain English, create monitoring plans, and review tracked patterns. It is not a diagnostic or emergency care service.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly in the app, including symptom descriptions, chat messages, health profile information, medications, allergies, uploaded medical record summaries, tracking entries, reminder preferences, and images you attach.

How we use information

We use this information to provide chat responses, research-backed summaries, monitoring plans, pattern summaries, account access, and reminder features.

AI processing

When you use AI-powered features, relevant data may be sent to the MyHealthPilot backend and to OpenAI in order to generate research translation, monitoring plans, and pattern summaries. Users are shown an in-app AI consent screen before these features are enabled.

Local storage

In this build, profile details, uploaded record summaries, chat state, reports, reminders, and tracking data may be stored locally on your device.

Account deletion

If you delete your account in the app, MyHealthPilot will attempt to delete your Firebase account and clear related local data stored on the device.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal health data. We may use service providers that process data on our behalf in order to operate app features.

Your choices

You can choose what information to enter, whether to upload records, and whether to use AI-powered features that rely on consent.
